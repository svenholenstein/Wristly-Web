Hey there! I'm Sven, a software developer based in Switzerland with a background in computer science and a deep passion for watches. Wristly started as a side project-something I built in my free time alongside my 9-to-5 job. As a true watch enthusiast, I wanted a simple yet powerful way to track my collection, analyze my wearing habits and bring some digital magic into the world of horology. Wristly was born from a simple idea: a small tool I originally built just for myself and a few colleagues to keep track of our watches. But what started as a personal project quickly grew into something bigger - a platform for everyone who shares a passion for timepieces.

I've poured countless hours into Wristly because I'm a watch enthusiast myself Every feature and detail reflects my dedication to horology. With Wristly, you can manage your collection, track your watches, and keep your favorite timepieces perfectly organized. To me, Wristly is more than just an app - it's a true passion project, and it will always remain one. My goal is to create an intuitive, stylish, and genuinely useful solution for every watch lover, whether you're a collector, a dealer, or simply someone who appreciates the art of watchmaking. Thank you for being part of this journey. Wristly is built with passion - and I'm excited to grow it together With you!
