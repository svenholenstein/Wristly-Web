1. Introduction

Welcome to Wristly! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Wristly mobile application ("App").

By using Wristly, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. What Data We Collect

Wristly does not collect or store any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, contacts, or photos.

However, the app may collect the following non-personal usage data solely on your device:

  • App usage behavior (e.g., which features are used most)
  • Local device information (e.g., device model, OS version)
  • Logs of app interactions (e.g., buttons tapped)

All data remains local on your device and is not transmitted over the internet.

3. How We Use the Data

All data collected by Wristly is used locally on your device to:

  • Enable core app functionality (e.g., watch statistics, usage tracking)
  • Improve user experience (e.g., remembering settings or watch data)
  • Show optional summaries or statistics

We do not use your data for marketing, profiling, or advertising.

No data is transferred to third parties or remote servers.

4. Data Storage and Retention

  • All data is stored locally on your device.
  • Wristly does not use any servers or cloud storage.
  • No internet connection is required to use the app.
  • You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the app or using in-app options.

5. In-App Purchases

Wristly offers one-time in-app purchases to unlock premium features. Purchases are handled through the App Store / Play Store and are subject to their respective privacy and payment policies. Wristly does not receive any personal information related to your purchase (such as your name, email, or payment method).
