Data Privacy

Last Updated: 03.02.2025

1. Collection and Use of Data

We collect and use personal data to provide you with the features of Wristly and improve your experience. The following data is collected:

  • Watch Data: Brand, model, reference number, and timestamps of the worn watch.
  • Usage Data: Information about your usage behavior, such as which features you use.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

The collected data is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve the app: We use your data to enhance the performance and user experience of the app.
  • Storage of watch data and timestamps: The data is stored on your device and helps you track your watch history.

3. Storage and Protection of Your Data

Your data is stored locally on your device. We do not store personal data on our servers. Your data is safe and protected, as we do not share it with third parties unless required by law.

4. Data Access and Deletion

You have the right to view, modify, or delete the data we store at any time. You can do this via the privacy settings in the app.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data. No personal data is shared with third parties unless necessary for service provision or required by law.

6. Consent to Data Usage

By using Wristly, you agree that your data will be processed in accordance with this privacy policy.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to change this privacy policy at any time. In the event of significant changes, we will notify you by publishing the updated policy on this page.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about our privacy policy or want to access, modify, or delete your data, you can contact us anytime at info@skycin.ch.
